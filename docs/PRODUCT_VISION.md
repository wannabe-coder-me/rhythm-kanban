# Entrepreneur's Life System - Product Vision

## Overview

A life navigation system for entrepreneurs based on the philosophy that the subconscious mind, when properly programmed through repetition, emotion, and visualization, will attract opportunities and guide actions toward achieving one's dreams.

**Core Belief**: "What the mind can conceive and believe, it can achieve."

---

## Philosophy & Principles

### The Four Types of People
1. **Droner** - Always complaining, drowning in problems
2. **Drifter** - "Whatever will be, will be" - no direction
3. **Surfer** - Enjoying life but going nowhere specific
4. **Navigator** - Has charts, maps, destination - this is our user

### The Iceberg Mind
- **Conscious Mind (10%)** - The "monkey mind", jumps from thought to thought
- **Subconscious Mind (90%)** - Doesn't judge, takes everything literally, controls automatic behaviors

### Programming the Subconscious
1. **Repetition** - Daily review of goals
2. **Emotion** - Feel it as if already achieved
3. **Visualization** - The mind can't tell imagination from reality
4. **Positive Language** - Mind doesn't understand "don't"

- "I want it because I want it" - no justification needed
- "Who must I become to achieve this?"
- "Abundance is not zero-sum"
- "We don't make money, we EARN it in proportion to value created"

## Core Features

### 1. Vision Board (per pillar)

**Purpose**: Capture dreams without judgment or "how"

**Components**:
- Free-form text entries: "What do I really want?"
- Optional image attachments (visualization)
- Time horizons: 1-year, 3-year, 5-year
- No limits, no logic required

**UX**:
- Card-based layout
- Drag to reorder by priority
- Color-coded by time horizon
- Optional vision board collage view

### 3. Daily Rituals

**Morning Ritual** (5-10 min):
1. **Gratitude** - 5 things grateful for today
2. **Pride** - 5 things proud of (builds confidence)
3. **North Star Review** - Read your card with EMOTION
4. **Identity** - "Who must I become today?"
5. **Top 3 Actions** - What will I do TODAY toward my goals?

**Evening Ritual** (5 min):
1. **Wins** - What went well today?
2. **North Star Review** - Read card again
3. **Tomorrow's Actions** - Plan top 3 for tomorrow
4. **Reflection** - Any course corrections?

**Gamification**:
- Streak tracking
- Completion percentage
- Weekly/monthly consistency scores
